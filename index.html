About Our Charity

The ABU RASHID ISLAMIC FOUNDATION (ARIF), is a vibrant Charitable Society/Organization registered under the Registrar of Societies in accordance with the Section 4 of the Societies Act (Cap 108) of Kenya. We are dedicated to enriching the lives of Muslims in Kenya. Inspired by Islamic values and guided by the Qur'an and Sunnah, we promote social welfare initiatives, offer educational programs for all ages, and foster a sense of belonging for all. Through our various activities, we empower individuals to grow in their faith, connect with others, and contribute positively to society. Join us as we build a stronger, more compassionate Islamic community, together.

Our Mission

To Foster Islamic Spiritual Growth, Social Cohesion, and Economic Prosperity for All. We strive to create an inclusive environment that nurtures the well-being of individuals and communities through various initiatives that promote education, healthcare, and welfare.

Our Vision

To build a Just and Compassionate Islamic Society Guided by Islamic Values and Principles. We envision a community where every individual is valued and supported in their journey towards personal and spiritual fulfillment, grounded in the teachings of Islam.

Our Main Objective

To promote Islamic teachings and values, foster spiritual growth, and serve the community through charity, education, and social welfare programs. Our objective is to ensure that the benefits of our programs reach the underprivileged and marginalized, thus uplifting their lives and providing opportunities for growth.

Our Mission

Education

Religious and Educational Activities

  • * Mosque/Madrassa construction and maintenance: Building and maintaining places of worship and studies for the community.
  • * Religious education: Offering classes, seminars, and workshops on Islamic principles, history, and culture.
  • * Quranic studies: Providing programs for the memorization and recitation of the Quran.
  • * Islamic schools: Establishing and supporting schools that adhere to Islamic values and curriculum.
  • * Scholarships: Offering financial assistance to students pursuing Islamic studies.

Healthcare

Community Health Initiatives

  • * Orphan care: Supporting orphaned children through sponsorship, education, and living expenses.
  • * Poverty relief: Providing food, clothing, and shelter to those in need.
  • * Healthcare: Establishing clinics, hospitals, or supporting healthcare initiatives.
  • * Disaster relief: Responding to natural disasters and providing aid to affected communities.
  • * Community development: Implementing projects to improve the infrastructure and quality of life in underserved areas.
  • * Prisoner support: Visiting and assisting prisoners, providing legal aid, and facilitating rehabilitation programs.

Sustainability

Promoting Sustainable Practices

  • * Da'wah (outreach): Spreading Islamic knowledge and promoting understanding of Islamic values.
  • * Social justice: Advocating for the rights of marginalized groups and promoting social equality.
  • * Youth programs: Organizing activities and programs for young people to develop their skills and leadership potential.
  • * Family counseling: Offering guidance and support to families facing challenges.
